Precautions After Blood Donation

Drink Water

Drink plenty of fluids to stay hydrated after donating blood.

Take Rest

Rest for few hours and avoid heavy physical activities.

Avoid Smoking

Avoid Smoking or alcohol for atleast 24 hours after donation.

Eat Healthy Food

Eat Iron rich foods like spinach,fruits and vegetables.

Eligibility Criteria

Who Can Donate Blood?

  • Age between 18-65 years
  • minimum weight 50kg
  • Healthy with no serious illness
  • Hemoglobin level should be normal

Who Cannot Donate?

  • People with severe infections
  • Pregnant women
  • People With heart diseases
  • People with low hemoglobin

Benefits of Blood Donation

Save Lives

One Blood donation can save upto three lives.

Health Check

Blood donation includes a basic health screening.

Improve Heart Health

Regular donation may reduce heart diseases.
